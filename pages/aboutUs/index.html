About us

Our short story

Welcome to Armenia, where the passion for leather craftsmanship thrives. We embarked on this journey with a simple yet powerful vision - to bring the experience of genuine leather products to everyone in our country. From the finest shoes to stylish belts and everyday leather goods, we take pride in curating a collection that exudes quality, style, and timeless elegance.

Our Vision

At Armenia, our vision is to redefine the way people connect with leather products. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the unparalleled beauty and craftsmanship of genuine leather. Our aim is to create a gateway for our customers to indulge in the tactile pleasure, durability, and sophistication that leather offers. By bringing together a diverse range of high-quality leather goods, we aspire to become the go-to destination for those seeking authenticity, style, and an unforgettable sensory experience.

Our Approach

At Armenia, we blend tradition and innovation to offer you an exceptional shopping experience. Our approach revolves around meticulous attention to detail and a deep understanding of our customers' desires. We source our leather products from skilled artisans who have dedicated their lives to perfecting their craft. By collaborating with these talented individuals, we ensure that each piece in our collection reflects the passion and expertise that goes into its creation.
